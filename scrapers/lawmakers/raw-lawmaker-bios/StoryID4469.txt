Larry Brewster - Billings, House District 44, Republican. Born 1951 in Idaho Falls, Idaho; moved to Montana in 1975; vocational school for radio electronics; industrial electrician; retired manager of telecom department, NorthWestern Energy; U.S. Army 1969-73; Heights Task Force; Billings school board, six years; Billings City Council, 11 years; served in House since 2021; spouse’s name is Vonda; two children.
