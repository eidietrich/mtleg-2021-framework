Moffie Funk - Helena, House District 82, Democrat. Born 1954 in the District of Columbia; moved to Montana in 1995; B.A. in political science, University of California Berkeley; educators license from Carroll College; public school teacher; 2014 Montana History Teacher of the Year; served in House since 2015; a daughter-in-law, a son and daughter who are fifth-generation Montanans, and a grandson who is a sixth-generation Montanan.
