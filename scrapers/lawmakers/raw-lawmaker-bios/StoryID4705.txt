Willis Curdy - Missoula, House District 98, Democrat. Born 1948 in Hamilton; B.A. in history, M.Ed., University of Montana; wildland fire fighter (smokejumper, Forest Service pilot); Big Flat Irrigation District Commissioner 1983-88; Missoula Rural Fire District Board of Trustees 1990-94; flight instructor; part owner Curdy Farm LLC; member Western Montana Teacher Center; served in House since 2015; spouse’s name is Gloria Shook Curdy; two grown sons.
