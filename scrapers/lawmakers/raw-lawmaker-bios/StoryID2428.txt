Greg Hertz - Polson, Senate District 6, Republican. Born 1957 in Malta; B.A. in accounting, University of Montana; Certified Public Accountant; President and CEO Moody’s Market, Inc., retail grocery stores; member of Rotary; served in House 2013-2020; Majority Whip 2015-16; Speaker Pro Tempore 2017; Speaker of the House of Representatives 2019; served in Senate since 2021; spouse’s name is Kate; three children, Kristin, Matthew, and Adam.
