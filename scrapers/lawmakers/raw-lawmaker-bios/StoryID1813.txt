Ellie Boldman - Missoula, Senate District 45, Democrat. Born 1975 in Scottsdale, Arizona, raised in Boise, Idaho. Bishop Kelly High School graduate; B.S. in psychology, Boise State University 1997; J.D., University of Idaho 2001; attorney; NCSL National Board of Directors; AAUW National Board of Directors; Missoula Rotary; served in House 2011-18; served in Senate since 2021; spouse’s name is Tyler; three sons, Elliot (15), Evan (11), and Jackson (7).
