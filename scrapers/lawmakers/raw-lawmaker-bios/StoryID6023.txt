Denise Hayman - Bozeman, House District 66, Democrat. Born 1951 in Winnipeg, Canada; moved to Montana in 1986; attended Pasadena City College; development director; five terms on Bozeman School Board; member of Bozeman School Foundation; served in House since 2015; Vice-Chair of Energy, Technology, and Federal Relations Committee; instrumental in raising funds for the Willson Auditorium renovation; served in House since 2017; House Minority Whip 2019-20; spouse’s name is Michael Scott; two children.
