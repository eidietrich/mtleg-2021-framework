Terry Gauthier - Helena, Senate District 40, Republican. Born 1957 in Spokane, Washington; moved to Montana in 2004; high school graduate; three years of college, noncompleted degree; business owner of McDonald’s Helena, retired 2020 after 21 years; retired 22-year member of  U.S. Marine Corps; past board member YMCA Helena; avid pilot; enjoys motorcycling, hiking, camping, and spending time with his dog; Business Man of the Year 2018 Helena, Montana; served in Senate since 2017; spouse’s name is Somer; two children.
