Jim Keane - Butte, House District 73, Democrat. Born 1941 in Butte; Butte Central High School graduate; attended University of Hawaii, Montana Tech, Western Montana College, and University of Wyoming; retired; former business manager, Operating Engineers Local 400; Montana AFL-CIO past vice president; served in House 200107 and since 2017; served in Senate 2009-15; spouse’s name is Dolores; three children, Alisa, Kiely, and Kevin.
