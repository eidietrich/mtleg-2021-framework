David Bedey - Hamilton, House District 86, Republican. Born 1954 in Hamilton. B.S. in civil engineering, Montana State University; M.A. in national security and strategic studies, U.S. Naval War College; Ph.D. in space physics, University of Alaska Fairbanks; retired U.S. Army colonel 1978-2008; chief financial officer of small engineering firm since 2010; Hamilton school district trustee 2009-18; served in House since 2019; spouse’s name is Deborah; one child.
