Marta Bertoglio - Montana City, House District 75, Republican. Born 1969 in Deer Lodge; B.S. in political science, U.S. Air Force Academy 1991; space operations officer; broker/owner of Uncommon Ground, LLC; U.S. Air Force active and reserves 1991-2004; Montana City School Board Trustee 2012-2020; served in House since 2021; spouse’s name is Joe Chapman; two daughters, Marrin and Cecelia (Celi).
