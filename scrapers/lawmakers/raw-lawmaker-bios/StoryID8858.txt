Brad Tschida - Missoula, House District 97, Republican. Born 1954 in Minneapolis, Minnesota; moved to Montana in 1963; B.A. in education (history and political science), minor in math; 40+ post-graduate credits in education leadership from the University of Montana; semi-retired in 2017; member of the Exchange Club; served in House since 2015; House Majority Leader 2019-20; spouse’s name is Leslee; five children; eight grandchildren.
