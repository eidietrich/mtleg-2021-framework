Jeffrey Welborn - Dillon, Senate District 36, Republican. Born 1968 in Dillon; Beaverhead County High School 1987; B.A. in business communications, May Technical College; owner, trailer dealership; construction services; agriculture; professional auctioneer; potentate Bagdad Shriners 2014; past director Montana Farm Bureau; past director Montana Auctioneers Association; Majority Whip 2013; served in House 2009-16; served in Senate since 2017; spouse’s name is Marni Fifield; two stepsons, Kash and Wyatt Kemph.
