Kathy Kelker - Billings, House District 47, Democrat. Born in Cleveland, Ohio; moved to Montana in 1978; B.A. in English/philosopy, Hiram College; M.S. in special education, Montana State University Billings; Ed.D. in adult education with a concentration on special education, Montana State University; executive director of Billings Head Start; board member of Billings Clinic Foundation, St. John’s Lutheran Home Ministries, MSU Billings advisory board; 15 years as a school trustee for Billings Public Schools; YWCA Salute to Women; Child Advocate Award Billings Chapter of Rotary; Faculty Excellence Award at MSU Billings; served in House since 2015; spouse’s name is Paul; four children.
