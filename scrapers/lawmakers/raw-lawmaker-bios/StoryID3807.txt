Russ Tempel - Chester, Senate District 14, Republican. Born 1947 in Havre; technical degree in welding, Billings Vo-Tech; A.A. in business, Northern University in Havre; third generation farmer, also worked as an appraiser and in construction; served in U.S. Army; longest-serving Liberty County Commissioner 1999-2016; active in 4-H, church, HRDC, Sage Creek Water Board, and Montana Association of Counties; served in Senate since 2017; spouse’s name is Judy; four children; 11 grandchildren.
