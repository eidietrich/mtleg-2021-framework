Mike Cuffe - Eureka, Senate District 1, Republican. Born 1947 in Eureka;  Lincoln County High School 1965; B.A. in journalism, University of Montana 1969; retired lumber/plywood mill manager/owner; owner of advertising/publicity business; president of Pacific Northwest Economic Region; several Model T organizations; served in House 2011-18; House Majority Whip 2013-14; former chair House Consumer Counsel; served in Senate since 2019; vice chair of Senate Finance and Claims; vice chair Joint Long-Range Planning; spouse’s name is JoAn; son Matt, daughter Molly Jo.
