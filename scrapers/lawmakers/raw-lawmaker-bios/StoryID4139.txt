Dan Bartel - Lewistown, House District 29, Republican. Born 1957; moved to Montana in 1965; high school graduate; retired independent businessman; served in House since 2017; served on 2017 Section A General Government Committee and 2017 and 2019 House Appropriations Committee; 2019 and 2021 section A Appropriations chair; served on 2017, 2019, 2021 Legislative Audit Committee; spouse’s name is Danielle, three sons.
