Mike Fox - Hayden, Senate District 16, Democrat. Born 1959 in Fort Belknap Agency, Harlem; graduated Harlem High School; attended Eastern Montana College for four years - Non Grad; Tribal Councilman; retired military; Gros Ventre Tribe; U.S. Coast Guard 1977-81, Montana National Guard 1981-2002 in Montana National Guard; Fort Belknap Tribal Council 2009-13, reelected 2019; served in Senate since 2021; spouse’s name is Delina Cuts The Rope; three sons.
