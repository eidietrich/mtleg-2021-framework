Christopher Pope - Bozeman, Senate District 31, Democrat. Born 1952 in Biloxi, Mississippi; moved to Montana in 1991; B.A. in Spanish literature, University of Oregon 1976; M.A. in public and private management, Yale School of Management 1988; CCIM commercial Realtor; served in House 2015-16 and 2019-2020; served in Senate since 2021; spouse’s name is Maddy; two children.
