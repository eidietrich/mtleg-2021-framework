Cary Smith - Billings, Senate District 27, Republican. Born 1950 in Salt Lake City, Utah; moved to Montana in 1993; B.S. in psychology, University of Utah; retired retail management from Sears, Roebuck and Co.; served in House 2009-14; House Majority Whip 2011-14; served in Senate since 2015; Senate Majority Whip 2015-2020; spouse’s name is Susan; six children; 35 grandchildren.
