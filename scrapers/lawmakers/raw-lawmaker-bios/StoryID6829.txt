Dennis Lenz - Billings, House District 53, Republican. Born 1962 in Boise, Idaho; moved to Montana in 1983; certificate of ranch management from Texas Christian University; certificate of pastoral studies from Yellowstone Valley Bible Institute; crop insurance adjuster; ran family farm 1987-2019; retired Billings Fire Department captain for 24 years; House sergeant-at-arms 2014-15; served in House in 2013-14 and since 2017; spouse’s name is Deanna; two children, Danae and Dustin.
