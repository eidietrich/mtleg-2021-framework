Susan Webber - Browning, Senate District 8, Democrat. Born 1955 in Browning; MPA, political science, Montana State University; consultant, educator; member of the Blackfeet Nation; So Kapi Ka Ya Yis Skats Tsi - To Run A Good Race  - Blackfeet youth running organization; American Indian Caucus; Blackfeet Women’s Society; War Mother’s Society; served in House 2015-18; served in Senate since 2019; spouse’s name is George.
