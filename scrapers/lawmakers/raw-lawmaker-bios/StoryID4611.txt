Mary M. Caferro - Helena, House District 81, Democrat. Born 1959 in Whitefish; Whitefish High School graduate; attended Helena College; community organizer/advocate; served in House 2005-10 and since 2019; served in Senate 2011-18; four children, Margaret, Hallie, Joseph, and Kenji Swain; grandson Eamonn Gray Asay-Swain.
