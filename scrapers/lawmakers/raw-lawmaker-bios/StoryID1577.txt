Jason Ellsworth - Hamilton, Senate District 43, Republican. Born 1973 in Laconia, New Hampshire; moved to Montana in 1989; owner of Bitteroot Gun Garage; finance chair of Ravalli County Republican Central Committee; cochair of Montana Sportsman Caucus; son of Master Sergeant Wendell Ellsworth stationed at Malmstrom AFB; served in Senate since 2019; partner’s name is Lindsey Murold; three sons, Jeremy, Kyle, Jaimeson.
