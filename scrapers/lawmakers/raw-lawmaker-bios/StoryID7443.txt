Terry Moore - Billings, House District 54, Republican. Born 1952 in Billings; B.S. in business with accounting emphasis, Montana State University; Senior Executive Management Program, Columbia University; president and board member of ADF Foundation; retired chief financial officer for First Interstate BancSystem for 35 years; CPA at KPMG for five years prior to banking career; Yellowstone Boys and Girls Ranch; past boards Montana Family Foundation, St. Vincent Healthcare, Montana Board of Investments, Billings West Rotary, Special K Ranch, La Vie; served in House since 2019; spouse’s name is Tena; three children; five grandsons.
