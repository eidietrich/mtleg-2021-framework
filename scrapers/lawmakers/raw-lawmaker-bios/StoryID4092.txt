Sue Vinton - Billings, House District 56, Republican. Born 1956 in Boston, Massachusetts; moved to Montana in 1996; A.A.S. in human services, S.U.N.Y. Al; B.S. in criminal justice, Al University; paralegal certification; owner Vinton Construction Inc; Lockwood School Board of Trustees 2004-16; member of Montana School Board Association Board of Directors and Montana School Unemployment Insurance Program Board of Directors 2012-16; served in House since 2017; spouse’s name is Mike; four children; five grandchildren.
