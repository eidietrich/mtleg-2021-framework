Frank Smith - Poplar,  House District 31, Democrat. Born 1942 in Poplar; graduated high school in Poplar; NCO and EMT training, Air Force; retired small business owner; Fort Peck tribal council; Fort Peck and Sioux Assiniboine;  in U.S. Air Force 1964-69; Boys and Girls Club of Fort Peck Reservation; started Fort Peck ambulance service; served five years on the Fort Peck Tribal Council; served in Senate 2005-08 and 2017-2020; served in House 1999-2004, 2011-12, and since 2021; one son, three daughters.
