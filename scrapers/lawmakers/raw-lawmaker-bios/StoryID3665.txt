Diane Sands - Missoula, Senate District 49, Democrat. Born 1947 in St. Ignatius; Frazer High School graduate; B.A. in anthropology, University of Montana; graduate work in history and women’s studies from George Washington University; retired from the Historical Museum at Fort Missoula; American Association of University Women; cosponsor of the Women’s History Mural in the Capitol; served in House 1997-98, 2007-12; served in Senate since 2015, Minority Whip 2021; spouse’s name is Ann Mary Dussault, who was the first female Speaker of the House.
