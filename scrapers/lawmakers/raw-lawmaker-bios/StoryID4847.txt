Mary Ann Dunwell - Helena, House District 84, Democrat. Born 1954 in Wilmington, Delaware; moved to Montana in 2004; B.A. in speech/communication with emphasis on journalism and Japanese language; certifications in public health communications, crisis and emergency risk communication, leadership; retired from state government public information; board member of the Salvation Army; was on Helena Citizens Council; former union officer for Montana Deptartment of Environmental Quality; serves as Lewis & Clark Democratic Central Committee Precinct person; served in House since 2015; one son and daughter-in-law.
