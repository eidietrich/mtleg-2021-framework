Barry Usher - Billings, House District 40, Republican. Born 1965 in Chambersburg, Pennsylvania; moved to Montana in 2002; studied criminal justice/sociology at University of Maryland University College; U.S. Coast Guard 1983; owner of Beartooth Harley-Davidson, Buffalo Bill Harley-Davidson, Beartooth Pass Harley-Davidson, past DP Solutions, Maui Wishes and Adventures, and DGN NASCAR Racing; life NRA member; Big Sky Young Presidents Organization/World Presidents Organization; NFIB member; Metra Park president/board member; Red Lodge Area Chamber president; Red Lodge Area Economic Development Corp president; NCSL executive board; vice chair Law and Justice Committee; served in House since 2017; spouse’s name is Ann Marie; four children; five grandchildren.
