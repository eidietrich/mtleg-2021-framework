Katie Sullivan - Missoula, House District 89, Democrat. Born 1983 in Denver, Colorado; raised in Tucson, Arizona; moved to Montana in 2001; B.A. in human biology, University of Montana;  J.D., University of Montana School of Law;  LL.M. in intellectual property and information technology law, University of Colorado School of Law; solo patent and trademark attorney; part owner of small manufacturing business; served in House since 2019; spouse’s name is Phil Stempin.
