Kenneth Walsh - Twin Bridges, House District 71, Republican. Born 1954 in Stockton, California; moved to Montana in 1962; Twin Bridges High School; B.S., M.S. in agriculture education, Montana State University; currently a rancher; recently retired as president/CEO of Ruby Valley Bank; trustee Ruby Valley Hospital; Twin Bridges Rotary Club; served in House since 2021; spouse’s name is Mary Beth; two sons.
