Jill Cohenour - East Helena, Senate District 42, Democrat. Born 1966 in Havre; B.A. in chemistry, University of Montana; chemist at Montana State Environmental Laboratory; life member of the RMEF; president of the Federation of Public Health and Human Services, Local #4573; chair of the AFT Program and Policy Council for Public Employees;  trustee on Eastgate Water and Sewer Board; served in House 2003-10; served in Senate since 2015; spouse’s name is Joe; two daughters, Allysha and Taylor; one grandson, Matthew.
