JP Pomnichowski - Bozeman, Senate District 33, Democrat. Born 1967 in Great Falls; B.S., Montana State University 1991; technical writer/graphic designer; EMT/firefighter; radio and TV news producer/director; orthopedic surgery practice manager; Bozeman Board of Adjustment, Planning Board, Zoning Commission; Montana State Board of Labor Appeals 2011-12; only woman on the MSU Bobcats football gameday stat crew, 30 years; served in House 2007-10 and 2013-14; served in Senate since 2015.
