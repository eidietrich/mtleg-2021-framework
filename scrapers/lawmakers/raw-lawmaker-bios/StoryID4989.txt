Dave Fern - Whitefish, House District 5, Democrat. Born 1953 in Providence, Rhode Island; moved to Montana in 1988; attended Community College of Rhode Island and Ithaca College; small business owner; Whitefish School Board for eight terms; chairman, past president, board of directors of Montana School Board Association; CASA volunteer; served in House since 2017; spouse’s name is Heather; three children.
