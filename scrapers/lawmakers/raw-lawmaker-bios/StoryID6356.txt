Llew Jones - Conrad, House District 18, Republican. Born 1962 in Helena; Conrad High School graduate; B.S. in agriculture business and M.S. in applied economics, Montana State University; small business owner, farmer, and rancher; Conrad Chamber of Commerce past president; Pondera Coalition for Progress President; two terms as a Conrad City Councilman; served in House 2005-10; served in Senate 2011-18; Majority Whip 2013-14; served in House since 2019; spouse’s name is Carole; four children; seven grandchildren.
