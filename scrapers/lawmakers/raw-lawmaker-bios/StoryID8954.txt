Rynalea Whiteman Pena - Lame Deer, House District 41, Democrat. A.A. in early childhood education, Chief Dull Knife College; member of the Northern Cheyenne Tribe; Head Start transportation manager 17 years; board member of Chief Dull Knife College eight years; two separate terms on Northern Cheyenne Utility Commission; very instrumental in youth and community activities; strong supporter for MMIW/MMIP;  president of the Northern Cheyenne Tribe January 2019-November 2020; served in House since 2021.
