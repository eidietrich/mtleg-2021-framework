Ed Stafman - Bozeman, House District 62, Democrat. Born 1954 in Key West, Florida; moved to Montana in 2008; B.A. in math, State University of New York at Stoney Brook 1975; J.D., Florida State University College of Law 1977; completed coursework for Ph.D. in religion, Florida State University 2007; Rabbinic ordination, ALEPH Rabbinic Program 2009; Rabbi Emeritus; attorney; served in House since 2021; spouse’s name is Beth; two grown children, Laura and Logan.
