Mike Lang - Malta, Senate District 17, Republican. Born 1949 in Fort Belknap; diploma from Malta High School; Carroll College; B.S. in business administration, Eastern Montana College; agribusiness owner; certified crop advisor; local chamber and agriculture; economic development; Knights of Columbus; 2015 Chair of House Energy, Technology, and Federal Relations Committee; Legislative Interim Energy, Economic Affairs, Environmental Quality and Montana Sage Grouse Oversight Team; Progressive Common Sense; 2019 chairman of Local Government Committee; 2021 chairman of Agriculture Committee; served in House 2013-16; served in Senate since 2017; spouse’s name is Lorna; three children, Micah, Lisa, and Lindsay.
