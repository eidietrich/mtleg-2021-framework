Sharon Stewart Peregoy - Crow Agency, House District 42, Democrat. Born in Crow Agency; Hardin High School graduate; B.S. in elementary education, Montana State University; M.Ed. specializing in curriculum and instruction, Seattle City University; 39-year career in pre-K and K-16 education; faculty at Little Big Horn College; enrolled member of Crow Tribe; founded the Apsaalooké Youth Leadership Cadre; fluent speaker of Crow language; served in Senate 2009-16; served in House since 2017; one daughter, Catherine “Buffy” Stewart.
