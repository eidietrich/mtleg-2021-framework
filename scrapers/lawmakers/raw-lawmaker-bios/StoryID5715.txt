Steve Gunderson - Libby, House District 1, Republican. Born 1957 in Seattle, Washington; moved to Montana in 1964; Libby Senior High School graduate 1975; retired small business owner, mining contractor, retail sales, electronics tech, and fencing contractor; Staff Sergeant E-6 in Montana Army National Guard and North Dakota Army National Guard for eight years; cofounder and 13-year spokesman for Montanore Positive Action Committee; served in House since 2017; spouse’s name is Cherie; two sons; two grandchildren.
