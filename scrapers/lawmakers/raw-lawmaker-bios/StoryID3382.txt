Ryan Osmundson - Buffalo, Senate District 15, Republican. Born 1979 in Oelwein, Iowa; moved to Montana in 1997; home educated; Montana Wilderness School of the Bible (one year); Colorado AeroTech; licensed ANP; rancher; farmer; agriculture consulting business; self-employed; served in the House 2011-16; Vice Chair of House Appropriations 2015; Chair of Senate Finance and Claims since 2019; served in Senate since 2017; spouse’s name is Jessica; ten children.
