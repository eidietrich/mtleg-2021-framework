Mary McNally - Billings, Senate District 24, Democrat. Born 1955 in Springfield, Massachusetts; moved to Montana in 1987; B.S. in urban studies, Worcester State College; MBA from Indiana University; Ph.D. in economic geography from the University of Chicago; business professor at MSU-Billings; Emeritus Professor, MSU-Billings College of Business; served in House 2011-14; served in Senate since 2015; spouse’s name is Monte Smith.
