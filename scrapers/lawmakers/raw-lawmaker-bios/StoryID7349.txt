Fiona Nave - Columbus, House District 57, Republican. Born 1954 in Manitowoc, Wisconsin; moved to Montana in 1959; Park High School, Livingston; B.S. California Polytechnic University, Pomona; operations research analyst for U.S. Navy; chairman, Stillwater Republican Central Committee; member, Stillwater Ambulance District #1 board; served in House since 2021; spouse’s name is Brent; one daughter, one son.
