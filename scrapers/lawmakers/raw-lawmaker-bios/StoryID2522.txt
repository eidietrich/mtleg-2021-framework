Brian Hoven - Great Falls, Senate District 13, Republican. Born 1941 in Great Falls; Great Falls High School graduate; B.S. in engineering, Princeton University; president of Hoven Equipment Co.; Army Reserve veteran; Rotary; director of Montana Equipment Dealers Assocation, GFC-MSU development board, Great Falls Development Authority, Black Eagle Park board; served in House 2009-14; served in Senate since 2015; spouse’s name is Barbara, married 54 years; two children; five grandchildren.
