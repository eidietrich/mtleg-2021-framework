Jimmy Patelis - Billings, House District 52, Republican. Born 1959 in Bethlehem, Pennsyl-vania; moved to Montana in 1980; B.S., Montana Tech 1984; A.A., Montana Tech 1982; federal courts three-year leadership program in criminal justice, Washington D.C. 1995; retired Chief U.S. probation officer; elected to the Montana Tech Football Hall of Fame 2018; served in House 201718 and since 2021; spouse’s name is Carin; two children.
