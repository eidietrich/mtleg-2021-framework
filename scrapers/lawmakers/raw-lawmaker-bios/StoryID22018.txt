Jonathan Windy Boy - Rocky Boy, House District 32, Democrat. Born 1958 in Fort Belknap; studied telecommunications at South-western Polytech Institute in Albuquerque, New Mexico; professional dancer, singer, policy advisor, public motivational speaker; Chippewa Cree, Assiniboine; sponsored legislation for language preservation language immersion laws and Zortman/Landusky gold heap leach mine reclamation; served in House 2003-08 and since 2017; served in Senate 2009-16; one child; four granddaughters, three grandsons.
