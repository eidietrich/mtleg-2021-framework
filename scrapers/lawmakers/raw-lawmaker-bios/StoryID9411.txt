Mark Blasdel - Kalispell, Senate District 4, Republican. Born 1976 in Kalispell; B.A. in hospitality administration, University of Nevada, Las Vegas; restaurant and catering owner; served in House 2007-14, chair of House Taxation Committee 2011-12, Speaker of the House 2013-14; Senate Pro Tempore 2019; served in Senate since 2015; spouse’s name is Renae; two children, Elizabeth Mae and Isaac Wade.
