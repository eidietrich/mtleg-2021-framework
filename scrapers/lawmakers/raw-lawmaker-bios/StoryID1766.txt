Duane Ankney - Colstrip, Senate District 20, Republican. Born 1946 in Idaho; moved to Montana in 1969; Lapwai, Idaho High School graduate; served in U.S. Navy 1964-69; retired coal mine processing superintendent; Mason, Farm Bureau, American Legion member, National Coal Council member; served in House 2005-14; served in Senate since 2015; spouse’s name is Carol Schillinger Ankney; five children.
