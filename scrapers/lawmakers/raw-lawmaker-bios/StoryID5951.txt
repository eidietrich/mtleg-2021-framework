Ed Hill - Havre, House District 28, Republican. Born 1959 in Havre; Havre High School graduate; A.A. in welding, Johnson County Community College; water service foreman; retired from BNSF RR; Officer Candidate School graduate in Montana Army National Guard; ski patrol 21 years; church council three years; school board trustree seven years; served in House since 2021; spouse’s name is Heidi, married since August 1991; one daughter.
