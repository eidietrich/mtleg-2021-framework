Steve Hinebauch - Wibaux, Senate District 18, Republican. Born 1955 in Havre; Turner High School graduate 1973; graduated Platte Valley Bible College, Scottsbluff, Nebraska 1977; rancher; served in Senate since 2017; spouse’s name is Beth, married since 1976; five married children; 17 grandchildren, one grandson-in-law, one great-granddaughter.
