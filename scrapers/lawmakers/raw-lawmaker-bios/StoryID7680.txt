Joe Read - Ronan, House District 93, Republican. Born 1955 in Ronan; studied agricultural mechanics at Montana State University Northern; A.A. in forestry, Salish Kootenai College; farmer/small cattle operation; large fire and disaster response; Eagle Scout; grew up in the Shriners Hospital in Spokane due to being born without hip sockets; served on Judiciary, Health and Human Services, Agriculture, and State Tribal interim committees; served in House 2011-12 and since 2019; spouse’s name is Jill; three sons, two daughters; four grandchildren.
