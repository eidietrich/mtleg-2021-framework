Mark Sweeney - Philipsburg, Senate District 39, Democrat. Born 1959 in Butte; B.S. in natural resource management, University of Montana Western 1982; real estate broker/owner; retired FWP; two terms Anaconda Deer Lodge County Commission; served in House 2019-2020; served in Senate since 2021; spouse’s name is Sue; two grown daughters, two stepdaughters.
