Kenneth Holmlund - Miles City, House District 38, Republican. Born 1948 in Miles City; B.A. in secondary education and a minor in math; M.A. in social studies; attended Miles City Community College and Eastern Montana College; retired locksmith after 30 years; Kiwanis member for over 30 years, including past Montana District Governor for 2001-02; chairman of the board of a 501(c)(3) after-school program since 2009;  served in House since 2015; spouse’s name is Joyce; three children.
