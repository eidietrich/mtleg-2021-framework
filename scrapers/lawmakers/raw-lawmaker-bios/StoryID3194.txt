Shannon O’Brien - Missoula, Senate District 46, Democrat. Born 1968 in Portland, Oregon; moved to Montana in 2008; Ph.D. in education leadership, University of Montana; M.A., Gonzaga University; B.A. in political science, University of Washington; high school teacher; Dean of Missoula College; Missoula Women’s Giving Circle; member of St. Francis Xavier Church; education policy advisor to Governor Bullock; served in Senate since 2021; spouse’s name is Chuck Dumke; one son, Carter.
