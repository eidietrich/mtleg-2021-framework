Marvin Weatherwax - Browning, House District 15, Democrat. Born 1966 in Butte; A.A.S. in network system support, Blackfeet Community College; Blackfeet language coursework for Class 7 teaching certificate; tribal councilman; Blackfeet tribal affiliation; veteran of U.S. Marine Corps; first in family to serve in state government since grandfather JD Weatherwax in the 1800s; served in House since 2019; spouse’s name is Melissa; four sons, three daughters.
