Tom France - Missoula, House District 94, Democrat. Born 1951 in Minneapolis, Minnesota; moved to Montana in 1972; J.D., University of Montana School of Law; retired regional executive director, National Wildlife Federation; Montana Environmental Quality Council 1983-91; board of directors of North Dakota Natural Resource Trust, Save our Wild Salmon, Clark Fork Coalition; served in House since 2021; spouse’s name is Meg Haenn; three children.
