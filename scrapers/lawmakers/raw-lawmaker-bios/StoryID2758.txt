Tom Jacobson - Great Falls, Senate District 11, Democrat. Born 1967 in Randal, Iowa; moved to Montana in 1989; B.A. in business management, University of Great Falls; M.P.A., University of Wyoming; executive director of Rural Dynamics, Inc., a nonprofit organization focused on helping all people achieve financial security; served in House 2013-18; served in Senate since 2019; two children.
