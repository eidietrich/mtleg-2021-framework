Linda Reksten - Polson, House District 12, Republican. Born 1951 in Polson; Ed.D. in teaching studies/education administration, UCLA; M.A. in education administration, Point Loma Nazarene University; B.S. in secondary education, biology major/chemistry minor, Eastern Montana College; Superintendent of Schools in Butte and Polson; member of Greater Polson Community Foundation, Lake County Republican Women, Pachyderm Club; written two books for Corwin Press on technology and student achievement; honored by President Bush for high student achievement at Disney Elementary, Burbank, California; served in House since 2021.
