Tyson Running Wolf - Browning, House District 16, Democrat. Born 1974 in Browning; Browning High School graduate; B.S. in forest resource management, University of Montana; forest resource manager - fire management officer; Blackfeet tribal affiliation; four-year Tribal Council member for the Blackfeet Nation; served as secretary on the executive board for Blackfeet Nation; served in House since 2019; spouse’s name is Lona; five daughters, two sons; three grandchildren.
