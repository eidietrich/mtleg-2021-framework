Edie McClafferty - Butte, Senate District 38, Democrat. Born 1960 in Butte; B.S. in elementary minor in reading and special education, University of Montana; M.A. in Educational Leadership - Administration; fifth-grade elementary teacher; Butte-Silverbow County Commissioner 1992-2001; finance and budget chair; currently serves on Butte-Silverbow Fire Commission; K-12 Data Task Force; served in House 2009-16; Minority Whip 2013-14; vice chair Education 2011-16; served in Senate since 2017; spouse’s name is Danny; three children; Amber, Jenny, and John.
