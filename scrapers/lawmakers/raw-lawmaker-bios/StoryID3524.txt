Keith Regier - Kalispell, Senate District 3, Republican. Born 1951 in Nebraska, moved to Montana in 1975; B.S. in education, University of Nebraska; retired teacher; West Valley School Trustee 1992-98; Flathead High School trustee 1999-08; served in House 2009-16; Majority Leader 2015-16; served in Senate since 2017; spouse’s name is Jolene; three children.
