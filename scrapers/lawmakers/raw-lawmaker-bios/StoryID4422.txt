Laurie Bishop - Livingston, House District 60, Democrat. Born 1970 in Rochester, New York; moved to Montana in 1996; B.A. sociology, Syracuse University; director, Montana Afterschool Alliance; former board member of Park County Community Foundation, Livingston Chamber of Commerce; local government study commission, city of Livingston 2005; currently member of Equal Pay for Equal Work Task Force and a Commissioner for Western Interstate Commission for Higher Education (WICHE); served in House since 2017; spouse’s name is Storrs; three children.
