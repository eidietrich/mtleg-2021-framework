Gregory Frazer - Deer Lodge, House District 78, Republican. Born 1983 at Cannon Air Force Base, Clovis, New Mexico; moved to Montana in 1984; graduated from Powell County High School; U.S. Army Reserve 2000-08, ammunition specialist, honorably discharged; mental health technician; one of two drummers for Walking Corpse Syndrome; served in House since 2021; three children.
