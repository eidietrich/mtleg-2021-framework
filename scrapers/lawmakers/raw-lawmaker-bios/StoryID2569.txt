David Howard - Park City, Senate District 29, Republican. Born 1946; B.S. in criminology, University of Los Angeles; M.S. in public administration, DePaul University; former FBI agent and BLM Chief of Law Enforcement officer for California; created, operated, and sold an investigative business; CEO of A&E Groups, a business that creates systems to prevent unethical workplace actions and provides training in workplace violence and communications; served in U.S. Army 1967-69, Vietnam veteran; served in House 2009-14; served in Senate since 2015; spouse’s name is Carla; six daughters, Cameo, Lacy, Julina, Dara, Karissa, and Arieana; 17 grandchildren.
