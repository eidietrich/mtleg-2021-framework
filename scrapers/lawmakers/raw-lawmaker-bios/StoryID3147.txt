Shane Morigeau - Missoula, Senate District 48, Democrat. Born 1984 in Missoula; L.L.M. from University of Arizona, James E. Rogers College of Law; J.D. from University of Montana School of Law; B.S. from University of Montana, College of Forestry Conservation; attorney; member of Confederated Salish and Kootenai Tribes; board member, All Nations Health Center; served in House 2017-2020; served in Senate since 2021; spouse’s name is Jamie Iguchi.
