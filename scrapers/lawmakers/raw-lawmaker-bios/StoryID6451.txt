Mike Hopkins - Missoula, House District 92, Republican. Born 1984 in Helena; degree in public administration, University of Montana; management consultant; member of Phi Delta Theta at the University of Montana; business manager, ASUM 2013-14; chairman, Montana Federation of College Republicans 2013-14; president, University of Montana College Republicans 2012-13; served in House since 2017; spouse’s name is Leah.
