Becky Beard - Elliston, House District 80, Republican. Born 1960 in Lewistown; B.A. in International Relations, Carroll College 1991;  Beard Environmental & Technical Assistance, LLC, Owner/Partner; associate member, Rocky Mountain Stockgrowers Association; previous Powell County School District No. 27 Trustee; served in House since 2017; Majority Whip for the 66th Legislature; spouse’s name is Alden; daughter, Jessica, resides with her husband in Arizona.
