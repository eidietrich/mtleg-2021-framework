Denley Loge - Saint Regis, House District 14, Republican. Born 1950 in Valley City, North Dakota; moved to Montana in 1964; St. Regis High School graduate; B.A. in geography, University of Montana; rancher; retired highway maintenance/construction; AFSCME highway policy chairman; Missoula/Mineral Farm Services Agency; Mineral County Soil Conservation Board; Mineral County Forest Coalition; Sanders County Collaborative; Mineral County Planning Board; UM Alumni Band; Governor’s Private Land/Public Wildlife Council; Region 2 FWP Citizens Advisory Committee; Trinity Lutheran Church; Western Legislators Academy 2018; founding member Missoula Maggots Rugby Club; served in House since 2017.
