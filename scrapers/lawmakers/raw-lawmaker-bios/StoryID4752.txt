Geraldine Custer - Forsyth, House District 39, Republican. Born 1955 in Miles City; fourth generation Montanan; Melstone High School graduate 1973; Eastern Montana College; Rosebud County Clerk and Recorder 1979-2014 (retired); past president Montana Association of Clerks and Recorders; past Rosebud Community Hospital Trustee; study commissioner; past Montana Election Task Advisory Council to Secretary of State; member of Energy Share of Montana and of Montana Council of Developmental Disabilities; served in House since 2015; two daughters, Amy and Arica; twin grandsons, Evann and Jaxson.
