Scot Kerns - Great Falls, House District 23, Republican. Born 1986; attended Randolph-Macon Academy, a military boarding high school in Virginia; attended U.S. Air Force Academy; B.A. in theology with minor in history, Concordia University Chicago; M.Div., Concordia Theological Seminary; currently earning MBA, University of Montana College of Business; former Captain in U.S. Air Force; currently Christian clergy; volunteer firefighter; longtime Civil Air Patrol member; Eagle Scout; American Legion member; served in House since 2021.
