Frank Garner - Kalispell, House District 7, Republican. Born 1962 in Independence, Miss-ouri; moved to Montana in 1970; Flathead High School graduate; A.A. in social justice, Flathead Valley Community College; retired Chief of Security for Kalispell Regional Healthcare; member of Rotary and Lions clubs; Great Chiefs Award (Kalispell Chamber of Commerce); Distinguised Alumni Award from Flathead Valley Community College; Melvin Jones Fellowship from Lions International; served in House since 2015; spouse’s name is Teresa; three children.
