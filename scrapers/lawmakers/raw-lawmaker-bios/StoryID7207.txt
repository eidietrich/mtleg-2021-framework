Bill Mercer - Billings, House District 46, Republican. Born 1964 in Billings; B.A. in political science, University of Montana 1986; M.P.A., John F. Kennedy School of Government, Harvard University 1988; J.D., George Mason University School of Law 1993; attorney, Holland & Hart, LLP; United States Attorney, Montana, 2001-09; Principal Associate Deputy Attorney General, U.S. Department of Justice, 2005-06; Acting Associate Attorney General, U.S. Department of Justice, 2006-07; served in House since 2019; spouse’s name is Marci; two children.
