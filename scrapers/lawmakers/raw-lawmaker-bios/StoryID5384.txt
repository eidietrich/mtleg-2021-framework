John Fuller - Kalispell, House District 8, Republican. Born 1946 in Glendale, California; moved to Montana in 1995; B.A. in history and economics, Northern Illinois University; M.S. in education, National Louis University; retired high school teacher; U.S.Army, Vietnam veteran 1967-71; Montana Board of Public Education 2001-08; served in House since 2019; spouse’s name is Cynthia; one child.
